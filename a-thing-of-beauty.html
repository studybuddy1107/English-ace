A Thing of Beauty

Summary

This poem by John Keats celebrates the everlasting nature of beauty. It explains how beautiful things provide joy and peace, lifting the human spirit even in tough times.

Important Questions

  • What does the poem say about the power of beauty?
  • How does beauty affect human life according to the poem?
  • What examples of beauty are mentioned?

Key Themes

  • Beauty and its permanence
  • Joy and peace
  • Nature and art
