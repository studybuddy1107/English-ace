Aunt Jennifer’s Tigers

Summary

This poem by Adrienne Rich depicts Aunt Jennifer’s struggle with her oppressive marriage and her expression of freedom and courage through the tigers she embroiders on a panel. The tigers symbolize strength and fearlessness, contrasting Aunt Jennifer’s own constrained life.

Important Questions

  • What do the tigers in the poem symbolize?
  • How does Aunt Jennifer express her feelings through her embroidery?
  • What does the poem say about the role of women in society?

Key Themes

  • Oppression and struggle
  • Freedom and courage
  • Feminism and gender roles
