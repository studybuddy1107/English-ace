An Elementary School Classroom in a Slum

Summary

This poem by Stephen Spender highlights the harsh conditions faced by children in a slum school. The poem contrasts the privileged children who attend good schools with the poor children who lack basic facilities. It appeals for empathy and social justice.

Important Questions

  • What differences does the poet highlight between privileged and poor children?
  • How does the poem depict the classroom in the slum?
  • What social message does the poet convey?

Key Themes

  • Social inequality
  • Poverty and deprivation
  • Empathy and justice
