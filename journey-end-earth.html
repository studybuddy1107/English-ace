Journey to the End of the Earth

Summary

This chapter recounts the author's journey to Antarctica, highlighting its pristine environment and the urgent need for ecological awareness. The narrative emphasizes how small changes in the ecosystem can lead to global consequences.

Important Questions

  • Why does the author visit Antarctica?
  • What lessons can students learn from such expeditions?
  • How does the chapter raise awareness about climate change?

Key Themes

  • Climate change and environment
  • Ecological balance
  • Scientific awareness
