The Rattrap

Summary

“The Rattrap” by Selma Lagerlöf is a story about a poor tramp who survives by setting rattraps and living a lonely life. He is invited into the home of a kind ironmaster who shows him unexpected kindness. The tramp experiences redemption and hope, realizing the “rattrap” life of cynicism can be escaped through compassion.

Important Questions

  • What is the significance of the rattrap in the story?
  • How does the ironmaster’s kindness affect the tramp?
  • What message does the story convey about human nature?

Key Themes

  • Redemption and hope
  • Compassion and kindness
  • Human nature and social isolation
